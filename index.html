Sacred Sexual Empowerment
with Naomi Prema Devi

Join in an a safe, sacred workshop exploring the topic of Sacred Sexual Empowerment.This is an opportunity to release unhealthy beliefs around sex and intimacy and empower what it means to be in your innocent, authentic sexual expression.

Would you like to:

  • Feel sexually empowered
  • Let go of shame, fear and blockages around sex
  • Understand the difference between innocent and distorted sexuality
  • Release limiting belief systems
  • Experience love more fully in all aspects of your life
  • Learn about healthy boundary setting and conscious consent
  • Communicate and express yourself authentically
  • Understand how the sexual energy can be utilised for sacred purposes

During this workshop we will explore some or all of the above topics through discussion, exercises and the magic of mystery.
The space will be safe, sacred and is open to whomever feels the heart calling, whether your single, in relationship, a beginner or an expert. It is inclusive of all genders and orientations. No nudity.

Empathy and Vulnerability
with Jonathan and Michael Bean

Empathy, the ability to relate to another person’s feelings as if they were your own (without losing the “as if” quality), is connected with having a better life in a myriad of ways. Vulnerability, as championed by social scientist and author Brene Brown, is essential to the experience of intimacy and connection in human relationships.

This workshop is simple at its heart – each person has an opening to experience as much vulnerability as they choose to experience, and to receive as much empathy as they’re ready to receive. It opens up an opportunity to experience the risk and reward of being witnessed in our experience and provides a chance to question the story that we create about ourselves in difficult moments. Everyone will be given a chance to edge past their comfort zone and experience what it’s like to take a risk and to be deeply supported in that risk by their community and their peers. There are profound gifts in being vulnerable – this workshop is for anyone who wants to find out exactly what those are.

Laughter Yoga
with Liselle and Aspen

Laughter yoga (Hasyayoga) is a practice involving prolonged voluntary laughter. This practice is based on the belief that voluntary laughter provides the same physiological and psychological benefits as spontaneous laughter.

Laughter is easily stimulated in a group when combined with eye contact, 'childlike playfulness' and laughter exercises. Practicing this form of yoga brings more oxygen to the body and brain by incorporating yogic breathing.

Join Aspen and Liselle in this workshop filled with joy, blissful giggle and deep body laughs!
